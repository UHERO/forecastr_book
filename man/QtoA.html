Description

Aggregate from quarterly to annual frequency (superseded by tsbox::ts_frequency())

Usage

QtoA(ser_in, aggr = "mean")

Arguments

ser_in

the xts series to be converted (freq = q)

aggr

aggregate via mean (default) or sum

Value

converted xts series (freq = a)
